WebTech Project Proposal - Logan Hitchcock

What is your website about?

My website will aimed to be about exercising; mainly revolving around weight lifting. I will use different pages to outline exercises based on each muscle group.

What is the objective of your website?

The objective of my website is to give a one-stop shop for all ages to have a database of available exercises for each muscle group and a picture to demonstrate.

Who is your target audience?

My target audience is those who work out but do not neccessarily know exactly what to do in the gym.

What content do you plan to include in each page of your website?

  • A homepage outlining the different muscle groups
  • A different page for each muscle group
  • At least 5 exercises for each muscle group
  • Picture demonstrations for each of the exercises
